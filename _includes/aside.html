Invisible Directory gathers, organizes, and promotes beautiful projects that use the World Wide Web as a space to effectively communicate about important social, environmental, cultural, and economic issues.

Our project has been a collaborative effort, with contributions from both strangers and friends. We invite you to share and use this site for research, reference, or just emotional reflection, as it is a resource intended for public good.
